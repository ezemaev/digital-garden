title: Things are getting busy
---

I always knew and believed that everything that I have ever done in my life always has an impact. Nothing goes to vain. It all adds up.

I'll tell you what I mean by this. Your favourite Anime, or Marvel Character, or the obscure blog post you read on Socio-Economics.
ALL OF THIS. EVERYTHING. It has meaning.
I have bonded with people on the first time because of a cool reference I made. Not on the Internet forums. I'm talking about in real life. Strangers getting closer. I have always taken pride in the breadth and depth of my own experience I have had as a human.

But there's always a selfish aspect when it comes to this. Not selfish, but all these activities I've done by myself.
I have stories and plenty of stories. But I think it's time to move on from having these experiences by myself and start with a group of people.

Watch things with people, do things with people, listen and read and run and live life with people.

I am very excited to start this journey again.
Past mistakes and failures will always exist. And I will make more in the future.
But learn from the past, remember it, cherish it, and move on.
Onwards to new experiences with new people.

Build a lifetime of memories with people. With people who will be throughout your lifetime now.

ACT 1 of my life will conclude now. The real race, the real marathon of life, over the next couple of decades starts now.

From myself to myself. You got this :")
